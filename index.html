About LarsonRentalLLC

Our Story

Founded in 2023, LarsonRentalLLC began as a small music store with a handful of instruments available for rent. Over the years, we've grown into Southern California's premier event equipment rental company, serving musicians, DJs, event planners, and performers of all kinds.

Our Mission

We're committed to providing high-quality, well-maintained equipment at affordable rates. Whether you're a professional musician needing backline gear for a tour or a parent planning a memorable birthday party, we have the tools to make your event successful.

Why Choose Us?

  • Extensive inventory of top-brand equipment
  • Knowledgeable staff with industry experience
  • Flexible rental periods to fit your needs
  • Delivery and setup services available
  • Competitive pricing for both rentals and purchases

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your rental terms and conditions?

Our standard rental period is 24 hours, with options for weekly rentals available. All rentals require a security deposit and valid ID. Equipment must be returned in the same condition it was rented. Full terms are provided at the time of rental.

Do you offer delivery and setup services?

Yes, we offer delivery and professional setup within the Greater Los Angeles area for an additional fee. Our team can handle everything from simple equipment drop-off to full event production setup.

What happens if equipment is damaged during my rental?

Customers are responsible for any damage to equipment during the rental period. We offer optional damage waiver coverage that can be purchased to reduce your liability. All equipment is inspected before and after each rental.

Can I extend my rental period?

Extensions are possible based on equipment availability. Please contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled return time to request an extension. Additional rental fees will apply.

Do you offer discounts for long-term rentals?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for weekly and monthly rentals. Contact our team for custom quotes on extended rental periods.
